Camacrew Skatepark Berlin Buch - Germany

October 7, 2023

The skatepark in Berlin Buch is brand new and has a bowl and a street section. The bowl has coping all around. The whole area is quite nice as is surounded by greenery and trees. The street section has a half bowl which is not as high as the bowl itself, so you can practice some drop-ins there. A bit further up is a smaller quarter which is also good to practice some drop-ins. In the bank section you can practice your first slides.

A bit further away is a big supermarket where there is also toilet. Next to the super market is a small shopping area where you can also get some Döner Kebap. The closest S-Bahn station is S2.

I was there on a Saturday in October after lunch and it was still pretty chill. In the afternoon more scooter kids and BMX people arrived.

Photos are from August 2022 and October 2023.
