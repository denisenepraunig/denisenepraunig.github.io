Skatepark Berlin Bernau - Germany

The skatepark Bernau features a bowl with coping and sections without a coping and a roll-in. There is also a street section with a quarter and a bank. The skatepark was build by Populär Skateparks. When I visited the skatepark last year I didn’t have a board with me, so I can’t say any specifics about how the bowl rides. The park is new so the concrete was very smooth.

Camacrew Skatepark Berlin Buch - Germany

The skatepark in Berlin Buch is brand new and has a bowl and a street section. The bowl has coping all around. The whole area is quite nice as is surounded by greenery and trees. The street section has a half bowl which is not as high as the bowl itself, so you can practice some drop-ins there. A bit further up is a smaller quarter which is also good to practice some drop-ins.
